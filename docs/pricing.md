## A note on halal payment terms

- Prefer **fixed, fee-based pricing** (which we already use) over anything interest-bearing.
- **No late-payment interest.** If you need to encourage on-time payment, use clear terms and deposits, not riba.
- **50% deposit** to start; balance on delivery. Deposits are a permissible, normal way to secure work.
- Use payment processors on a **fee** basis (e.g. Stripe/Wise transaction fees), not interest-based credit arrangements.

---

## Rules of thumb

- **Always present 3 tiers.** Most buyers choose the middle.
- **Quote per project, never per hour.** Hourly punishes you for getting faster.
- **Raise rates** every 2–3 closed clients — your portfolio justifies it.
- **Anchor on ROI honestly:** "One extra conversion a month often covers the retainer" — only when the math is real for that client.
