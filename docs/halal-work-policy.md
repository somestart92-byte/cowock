# Halal Work Policy

Bayan Copy is a halal-aligned studio. This means two things: **what we write about**, and **how we persuade**. Both matter.

> Principle: *“Whoever deceives is not one of us.”* Honest persuasion isn't a constraint on the work — it's the brand.

---

## 1. Industries we don't write for

We decline projects whose core business is:

- **Alcohol, drugs, and intoxicants** — breweries, bars, vape/cannabis brands.
- **Gambling & betting** — casinos, sportsbooks, lotteries, betting apps.
- **Riba (interest-based finance)** — conventional interest-bearing lending, payday loans, interest-based credit cards, conventional insurance built on riba/gharar. (We *do* serve Sharia-compliant finance — see below.)
- **Adult / immodest content** — pornography, escort services, immodest imagery.
- **Pork and non-halal meat** as the core product.
- **Anything promoting the above** — affiliate/ads businesses whose revenue depends on these.

When in doubt, we ask a knowledgeable source rather than guess. A polite "this one isn't a fit for us" is always available.

---

## 2. How we persuade (honest by default)

Even for fully halal clients, the *method* stays permissible. We avoid:

- **Fake urgency / scarcity** — "only 2 left!" when it isn't true; countdown timers that reset.
- **Deceptive claims (gharar)** — promising outcomes we can't substantiate; hidden terms.
- **Manipulative dark patterns** — confirm-shaming, forced continuity, buried unsubscribe.
- **Exaggeration & false testimonials** — invented reviews, doctored numbers.

Instead we persuade with: a real understanding of the customer's problem, honest benefits, genuine proof, and clear next steps. This converts *better* over time because it builds trust — and it's something we can stand behind.

---

## 3. Clients we actively pursue (halal economy)

- **Islamic finance & halal fintech** — Sharia-compliant banking, halal investing, takaful, zakat tools.
- **Halal consumer brands** — halal food & e-commerce, modest fashion, Muslim lifestyle apps.
- **Education & non-profit** — Islamic education, courses, charities (sadaqah/zakat campaigns).
- **Permissible B2B SaaS & services** — any honest product that doesn't fall under section 1.

---

## 4. How we talk about it

On the site and in pitches, frame it positively:

> "We write conversion copy the honest way — no fake scarcity, no deceptive claims. We also don't work with alcohol, gambling, interest-based finance, or adult brands. If your product is good, we'll help more people see why."

This filters out the work we don't want and signals trustworthiness to the clients we do.
