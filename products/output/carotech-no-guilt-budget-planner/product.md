# The No-Guilt Budget Planner for Moms
### A kind, simple way to see your money clearly — without the shame

*A faceless CaroTech printable. Print at home, fill it in, breathe easier.*

**Suggested price:** $17

---

## How to use
Print what helps and start with the Monthly Budget Overview. Add a page when you're ready. This is about *knowing* your money, not restricting yourself.

## What's inside
1. **Monthly Budget Overview** — Three numbers tell the whole story: what comes in, what must go out, and what's left.
2. **Bill & Due-Date Tracker** — List every bill and check it off when it's paid. Calm beats late fees.
3. **Weekly Spending Log** — Jot every spend for one week. Awareness changes habits faster than willpower.
4. **Savings Goals** — Name the goal, set the target, color in your progress.
5. **Guilt-Free Money** — A small amount that's just yours — no justifying. Being in your own budget isn't selfish.
6. **No-Spend Challenge** — Pick your days. Color a square for each no-spend day. Note what you did instead.
7. **Grocery & Meal Budget** — Plan the week's meals and a grocery cap. Small wins here add up fast.
8. **Sinking Funds** — Save a little each month for the things you know are coming.
9. **Debt Pay-Off Tracker** — List what you owe and chip away. Smallest balance first for quick momentum.
10. **Monthly Review** — Be kind to yourself here. Progress, not perfection.

*A general budgeting resource for moms — not financial advice. No interest, loans, or credit are promoted; this is about your own money, plainly. Personal use only.*
