## 1. Budgeting (contrarian) — ✅ QUEUED
unpopular opinion: most moms don't need another budgeting app 📵

you need 3 numbers:
→ what's coming in
→ what HAS to go out (rent, food, bills)
→ what's left

that's it. write them on one sticky note. apps overcomplicate what a notebook does in 5 minutes.

start there this week — you'll feel more in control by friday.

## 2. Money guilt (nobody tells you) — ✅ QUEUED
nobody tells you the hardest part of money as a mom isn't the math — it's the guilt 💛

every "treat" feels selfish. every "no" feels mean.

try this: give guilt-free money a name. a small weekly amount that's just yours, no justifying.

tiny — but it changes how the whole budget feels. you're allowed to be in the plan too.

## 4. Get organized — ⏳ READY

momentum beats motivation. one finished drawer beats an overwhelming weekend you never start.

## 5. Screen-free (CTA) — ⏳ READY
the "i'm bored" summer survival trick that saved my sanity:

a "pick a card" jar. write 15 no-screen activities on slips. when the whining starts, they pick one. decision made, debate over.

takes 10 min to set up tonight. works all summer.

(i made a 129-activity version — it's in my bio if you want the easy button. 💛)
