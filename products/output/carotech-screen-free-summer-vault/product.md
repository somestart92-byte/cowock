## Welcome, Mama 💛

## Pack 1 — Backyard & Outdoor Boredom-Busters (20 activities)

*Print-and-go ideas that send them outside and burn energy.*

1. Nature scavenger hunt — find something rough, something that flies, a yellow flower, a heart-shaped rock.
2. Driveway chalk obstacle course — hop, spin, tip-toe along the chalk path.
3. Giant chalk tic-tac-toe — draw a grid, use rocks as markers.
4. Shadow tracing — trace your shadow at morning, noon, and evening; compare.
5. Sponge toss relay — soak sponges, race to fill a cup across the yard.
6. Fence “painting” — a bucket of water + a big brush = mess-free art.
7. Ice-cube rescue — freeze small toys, free them with warm water.
8. Backyard Olympics — long jump, bean-bag toss, balloon keep-up, medals.
9. Bug explorer log — find, draw, and count the bugs you spot.
10. Mud kitchen — old pots + dirt + water = an hour of “cooking.”
11. Kindness rocks — paint smooth rocks and hide them for neighbors.
12. Bubble station — mix dish soap + water; try giant-bubble wands.
13. Household obstacle course — hula hoop, broom hurdle, pillow leap.
14. Plant-a-cup garden — seeds in a cup, water daily, watch it grow.
15. Cloud-watching journal — draw the shapes you see in the sky.
16. Water-balloon target toss — chalk a target, aim and splash.
17. Nature mandala — arrange leaves, petals, and stones in circles.
18. Sprinkler dance / hose limbo — music optional, giggles guaranteed.
19. Sandbox treasure dig — bury coins or toys to excavate.
20. Backyard campout — build a fort, grab a flashlight, tell stories.

**What's printable:** Scavenger hunt cards, chalk challenge poster, Olympics scorecard, bug log.

> **Quick win:** Outdoor + a printed “mission” = 45 minutes of independent play.
