What kinds of businesses do you write for?

Almost any industry — SaaS, e-commerce, coaches and creators, local services, B2B. The only exceptions are alcohol, gambling, interest-based finance, and adult brands, which I don't take on.

Aggressive tactics can spike a short-term number while eroding trust. Honest, research-driven copy converts well and keeps converting — because your audience believes it. I write to a metric, so we measure the result either way.
