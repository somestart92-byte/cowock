# Sara AI — LinkedIn Outreach Kit (target: 2 clients before Monday)

Posts build credibility. **Direct outreach closes deals.** This is the closing engine.
Do NOT use automation tools — LinkedIn bans accounts for it. Send these by hand;
each takes about 20 seconds.

---

## STEP 1 — Find the right people (10 minutes)

In the LinkedIn search bar, search these one at a time:
- "dental practice owner"
- "principal dentist"
- "dental practice manager"
- "practice principal dentist UK"

Filter: People → Locations → United Kingdom.

Aim for owners / principals / practice managers of independent clinics (not big
chains like Bupa or mydentist — they decide slowly).

Target: send 15–20 connection requests today. Out of 20, ~8 accept, ~2 reply, and
that's where your clients come from.

---

## STEP 2 — Connection request note (paste, tweak the name)

> Hi [First name], I work with UK dental clinics on call answering and patient
> booking. Came across your practice and thought it'd be good to connect.

Keep it that short. No pitch in the connection request — pitching here kills accept
rates.

---

## STEP 3 — First message AFTER they accept (wait a few hours)

> Thanks for connecting, [First name]. Quick question — how does [Clinic name] handle
> calls when reception is busy or after hours?
>
> Reason I ask: we built an AI receptionist for dental clinics that answers every
> call, books appointments and handles patient questions 24/7. A few practices are
> using it instead of stretching their front desk.
>
> Happy to show you how it sounds if it's useful — you can hear it here:
> https://wonderful-meerkat-938250.netlify.app/

---

## STEP 4 — If they reply with interest

Switch to the CLOSING-PLAYBOOK.md scripts. The "How does it work", "How much",
and "Yes let's do it" replies all work the same on LinkedIn as email.

Always end with one clear next step: a 15-minute call.

---

## STEP 5 — If no reply after 3 days (one nudge only)

> No worries if it's not relevant, [First name] — just leave it with you. If you
> ever want to hear how Sara handles a real call, the demo line's always open:
> https://wonderful-meerkat-938250.netlify.app/

Then stop. One nudge, no more.

---

## Daily rhythm to hit 2 by Monday
- Today: 20 connection requests + send Step 3 to anyone who accepts.
- Tomorrow: 20 more requests, reply to every message within a couple of hours.
- Weekend: keep replying — practice owners often check LinkedIn off-hours.
- Speed of reply matters more than anything. Answer fast while interest is hot.

## Rules
- Everything you say must be true. No invented stats, no fake clients.
- One person, one conversation — never copy-paste blasts that feel robotic.
- Personalise the first line with their name and clinic.
- The goal of every message is the next step, not the sale.
