- **Contrarian & "nobody tells you" hooks** ("unpopular opinion", "nobody tells you")
  pull the most views — they out-perform plain tips.
- **"Start with zero money / this week"** framing (low barrier) resonates.

## Recommendations
1. **Lean into budgeting/money-for-moms** as a content pillar — and build a
   **budget-planner product** (the $9 planner post already got traction).
2. **Use contrarian hooks** more often: "unpopular opinion…", "nobody tells you…",
   "stop doing X".
3. **Low-barrier promises** ("this week", "zero money", "in nap-time") convert
   attention — keep them.
4. Post volume is fine; **engagement is the gap** → prioritize stronger first
   lines over more posts.
