# Sara AI — Closing Playbook (target: 2 clients before Monday)

Paste-ready replies. When a clinic replies by email or LinkedIn, match their message
to the closest scenario below and send. Keep it human — no bullet points, no hype.

---

## 1. "How does it work?" / "Tell me more"

Thanks for getting back to me.

It's simple — we plug Sara into your existing phone number. When a patient calls
and your team can't pick up (or it's after hours), Sara answers. She books the
appointment straight into your calendar, answers the usual questions about hours,
pricing and location, and texts you a summary of every call.

Setup takes about 48 hours and we do all of it. The first month is free so you can
hear her handle real calls before you pay anything.

Want me to set up a quick 15-minute call this week to walk you through it? What day
suits you?

---

## 2. "How much is it?" / pricing question

The Growth plan is £299 a month, which covers call answering, appointment booking
and reminders. There's a one-time setup fee of £999 — that covers building Sara
around your specific clinic, your services, your calendar.

The first month is free, so you only pay the setup, and you can cancel any time.

If it's useful I can show you exactly what's included on a short call — when are you
free this week?

---

## 3. "Send me a demo" / "Can I hear it?"

Of course — you can call Sara right now and hear exactly what your patients would
hear: https://wonderful-meerkat-938250.netlify.app/

Have a go, then tell me what you think. If you'd like, I'll set Sara up for your
clinic free for the first month so you can test her on real calls.

---

## 4. They're interested but hesitant / "let me think"

Completely understand — no pressure at all.

The reason I'd suggest trying the free month is there's genuinely no risk: you don't
pay the £299 until month two, and you can stop any time. The worst case is you've had
a month of every call answered for free.

If it helps, I can get you set up this week and you decide at the end of the month.
Shall I send over the setup details?

---

## 5. "Yes, let's do it" / ready to start  ← THE CLOSE

Brilliant — really glad to have you on board.

To get Sara set up I just need three things:
- Your clinic name and main phone number
- The calendar or booking system you use
- A good time for a 15-minute setup call this week

Send those over and we'll have Sara live within 48 hours. First month is free,
starting the day she goes live.

---

## 6. "STOP" / not interested

No problem at all — I've removed you and you won't hear from me again. Wishing you
and the practice all the best.

(Then mark them opt-out and never contact again.)

---

## Rules
- Reply within a few hours while interest is hot.
- One clear next step in every message (usually: book the call).
- Never invent features, stats, or claims. Everything above is true.
- Max 2 cold emails per clinic. Once they reply, the 2-email cap no longer applies —
  you're now in a conversation they started.
